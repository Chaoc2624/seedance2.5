## About Seedance 2.5

Seedance 2.5 is an AI creative workspace for people who turn prompts, references, and ideas into visual assets. The product focuses on video generation, image generation, model comparison, and practical workflows for creators, marketers, studios, and product teams.

## What We Build

We bring tools such as Seedance 2.5, GPT Image 2, Nano Banana, nanobanana, and other supported models into a focused production surface. The goal is to make it easier to test directions, compare outputs, refine prompts, and move from concept to publishable creative work.

## Our Principles

Seedance 2.5 is designed around clear controls, fast iteration, transparent costs, and useful creative history. We prefer workflows that help people make better decisions instead of hiding model behavior behind vague automation.

## Who It Is For

Seedance 2.5 is for creators who need short videos, campaign visuals, product images, ad concepts, character references, and style experiments without stitching together several disconnected tools.
