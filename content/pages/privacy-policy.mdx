## Overview

Seedance 2.5 provides AI video generation, image generation, model selection, and prompt-based creative workflows. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using Seedance 2.5.

## Information We Collect

We may collect account information such as your name, email address, authentication details, and billing status. When you use generation features, we may process prompts, uploaded references, generated outputs, model settings, task status, and usage metadata needed to run the service.

We also collect technical information such as browser type, device information, IP-derived region, log events, cookies, analytics events, and security signals. Payment details are processed by payment providers. Seedance 2.5 does not store full card numbers.

## How We Use Information

We use information to provide and secure the service, process AI generation tasks, maintain accounts, calculate credits, deliver support, prevent abuse, improve product quality, and comply with legal obligations.

## Uploaded Content and Generated Outputs

Your prompts, uploaded media, and generated outputs are used to provide the requested generation workflow. Do not upload content you do not have the right to use. We may retain task records and output metadata for history, billing, moderation, debugging, and reliability.

## Sharing

We share information only with service providers that help operate Seedance 2.5, including hosting, database, storage, authentication, analytics, email, payment, and AI model providers. We may disclose information when required by law or to protect users, the service, or the public.

## Cookies and Analytics

Seedance 2.5 may use cookies and analytics tools to keep you signed in, remember preferences, measure usage, and improve the product. You can control cookies through your browser settings, although some features may stop working.

## Security

We use technical and organizational measures designed to protect information. No online service can guarantee absolute security, so you should keep your account credentials safe and notify us if you suspect unauthorized access.

## Your Choices

You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of personal information where applicable. Some records may be retained when needed for security, legal, billing, or abuse-prevention reasons.

## Changes

We may update this policy as Seedance 2.5 evolves. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at [support@seedance-2-5.app](mailto:support@seedance-2-5.app).
